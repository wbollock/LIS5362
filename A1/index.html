Richard Stallman

Richard Stallman is a free software advocate, founder of the GNU project, and general technology guru. He is best known for being a pioneer for the GNU project.

Richard Stallman, also known as RMS, is a famous programmer that accomplished much in his early years. Recently he was plagued by a terrible scandal, but continues to comment on current issues in technology and around the world.

Accomplishments of RMS

  • GNU Project
  • A founder of Emacs
  • Creator of GNU make, and the compiler GCC
